# Assembly Reference

* `AND` - Bitwise AND
* `OR`  - Bitwise OR
* `XOR` - Bitwise XOR
* `NOT` - Bitwise NOT
* `SHR` - Shift right
* `SHL` - Shift left
* `CMP` - Compare
* `ADD` - Add 

## Example

```assembly
 ADD 1, 2 ; adds 1 to 2
 CMP 2, 3 ; compares 2 and 3
 SHR 2    ; shifts 2 to right
 AND 0,1  ; bitwise AND
 NOT 1    ; 1's complement
```
